Since 2024, I am a postdoc at ENSAE working under the supervision of [Anna Korba](https://akorba.github.io) on mutlimodal Bayesian sampling. Prior to that, I completed my PhD in 2023 on the closing of the statistical/computational gap in smooth Optimal Transport under the supervision of [François-Xavier Vialard](http://angkor.univ-mlv.fr/~vialard/).
